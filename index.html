Inspired by our love for sriracha 🌶️ and our deep roots in speciality coffee ☕ we created a hot sauce to end all hot sauces.

Coffee brings a natural fruity 🍒 sweetness to Koffiracha that creates a pleasant balance between flavour and heat.

Our single-origin coffee comes from diverse provenances 🏞️ and varieties to produce complex flavours that make every batch unique; some fruitier, while others nuttier or a bit more savoury.

Koffiracha's creamy texture coats your tongue 👅 and delivers a burst of heat. Then, a tangy acidity from the white wine vinegar and coffee sweetness lingers–finishing with smooth umami notes.
